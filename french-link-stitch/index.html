French Link Stitch

Bookbinding Stitch & Thread

This was my favorite book to make from the class. After the Ethiopian Book Binding, which I considered to be the most difficult binding, this was an easier and nicer change of pace. I was also extra satisfied with my work because I made it at home without the lecture (only with the Youtube video) because I was out of town attending a wedding. Also, I used copy paper as my cover paper because I was at home and did not have decorative paper.
