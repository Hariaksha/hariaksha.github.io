This was by far my favorite book that I have made so far. I enjoyed using the needle and thread to sew or stitch through the spine of the book much more than I like using tape, which was used in my flutterbook and accordion book. I like how the process felt iterative with nice, elegant rules, such as always wrapping around the spine at a new hole and traveling to the next hole until the book was complete. I made a few mistakes in my design for two books when I did not follow the patterns exactly, but I learned a lot and was able to fix my mistake faster than I thought I would have.
